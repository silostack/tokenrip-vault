# Closer Session — 2026-05-19 — Insurance LinkedIn outreach

**Mode**: Follow-Up Coaching (firm-direct, insurance vertical)

## What happened

Simon asked how to do LinkedIn outreach to accepted insurance connections, starting
with Mohammad "Sultan" Ahmed (NY Coverage LLC / SkR Agency Inc).

### Coaching points
- Pushed back on Simon's "lead with an offer" instinct. To a cold connection a
  vague offer is spam and a specific offer is a pitch to unvalidated pain
  (violates gameplan Hard Rule #1 in spirit). The real first-touch offer is a
  sharp question + the discovery posture.
- First read of Sultan's profile suggested "COI grind." Simon caught it: Sultan's
  three current roles are technical underwriting, coverage architecture, carrier
  negotiation, deep-dive policy reviews — a specialist, not a CSR volume shop.
  COI was a wrong-but-specific guess. Reframed onto policy-review/negotiation depth.
- Built a 3-bucket system so outreach scales without one-line spam: A (volume
  brokerage), B (technical specialist/principal), Fallback (can't tell → open).

### Deliverables
1. **Final message to Sultan** (Bucket B, humanized — no em dashes, no "curious"):
   > Thanks for connecting, Sultan. I build AI agents for insurance brokerages.
   > Not pitching you here. We're in discovery, looking for design partners, so
   > I'd rather hear where the hours actually go than sell anything. For
   > coverage-heavy work like yours, what eats the most time you'd never get
   > back? Policy reviews, carrier negotiation, something else? Happy to trade
   > notes on what we're seeing across other brokerages.
