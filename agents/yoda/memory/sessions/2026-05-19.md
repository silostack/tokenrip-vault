## Diagnosis

- **Doubt location:** Not the product, not ability-to-learn. It's an *outcome* doubt — "can we get distribution / find buyers / run a sales machine" — with a thin *worth*-thread underneath (three years without meaningful revenue).
- **Presence unknown:** Simon honestly can't tell if the doubt is active or something he's pre-empting. "A pimple I don't know is a pimple." This is why round one installs a detector before treating anything.
- **The corrupted dataset.** The doubt collapses two separate questions: "did the crypto project get traction?" (no — for nameable structural reasons) and "can Simon do distribution?" (unproven — the crypto experiment was confounded). It reads "the vitamin didn't sell" as "I can't sell."

## The Evidence Reframe

- Simon ran **300+ sales conversations** on the crypto project — real reps most doubting founders never have.
- He diagnosed the failure *structurally* (vitamin not painkiller, bank sales cycles, partnership gating, crypto penalty) and **repriced the whole company on it** — pivoted to AI. Reading the market correctly and acting on it is distribution literacy, not its absence.
- The "three years no revenue" sting was reframed as **tuition** — and Simon confirmed this is already his own frame ("I needed to go through that to get to the realizations I have now"). The frame is owned, not borrowed — so it can carry weight.
- Honest sample size on "Simon doing focused distribution" is ~5 weeks (May 8 sprint onward), not 3 years.

Two anchor affirmations carried now (evidence-true regardless of detector outcome). Visualization + full affirmation stack **deferred to round two** — calibrated after the detection log.
