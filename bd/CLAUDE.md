## Strategic frame

We're testing whether enterprise demand for a vertical AI workflow stack is real, fast. Tokenrip is the durable infrastructure layer; the application layer is built per-vertical. Lock one design partner, prove the motion, then decide whether to expand into a second vertical or double down on the first.
