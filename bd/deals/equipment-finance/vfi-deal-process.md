# VFI Deal Process

> **Source:** VFI Corporate Finance — Originations · Internal Reference
> **Product:** Direct Private Credit · $1MM–$50MM · Equipment & Project-Backed · US & Canada · Funds in 2–4 weeks

The full transaction lifecycle from first contact to maturity, end to end. Two phases: **Origination** (days to weeks) and **Asset Lifecycle** (months to years).

---

## Phase I — Origination

> Sales & Ops · Days to Weeks

### Stage 1 — Prospecting
**Owner:** BDO

Identify and qualify targets to the VFI box: revenue $30M–$1B+, equipment- or project-backed, US & Canada. Exclusions: cannabis, AR (accounts receivable), inventory, and real estate.

**Advances when:** A qualified financing need that fits the box.

---

### Stage 2 — Opportunity Created
**Owner:** BDO

Log the deal — size, use of proceeds, asset, sponsor. Run a preliminary credit-fit check and request financials.

**Advances when:** Enough in hand to structure and price.

---

### Stage 3 — Issue Term Sheet
**Owner:** BDO + Structuring

Deliver indicative, non-binding terms covering: structure, rate or factor, term, advance rate, fees, and conditions.

**Advances when:** Borrower signs the term sheet; application deposit paid.

---

### Stage 4 — Submit for Underwriting & Credit Decision
**Owner:** Credit

Full package submission: audited / CPA-reviewed financials, financial spreading, collateral review, KYC/AML, and committee or delegated-authority review.

**Advances when:** Approve with conditions, counter, or decline.

---

### Stage 5 — Prepare Documentation
**Owner:** Documentation

Paper the approved structure. Supported lease/loan types: EFA (Equipment Finance Agreement), $1 buyout, 10% PUT, FMV (Fair Market Value), or TRAC. Includes guaranties and UCC filings; all conditions cleared.

**Advances when:** Complete, accurate package ready to execute.

---

### Stage 6 — Prepare Authorizations to Fund
**Owner:** Funding

Collect executed docs. Confirm all conditions precedent. Verify delivery/invoice (or progress draw), insurance, and UCC. Internal sign-off required.

**Advances when:** All conditions precedent met and funding authorized.

---

### ★ Funded — Closed-Won

Capital disbursed. Deal books to the portfolio. **Origination complete.**

---

## Phase II — Asset Lifecycle

> Servicing & Asset Management · Months to Years

### Stage 7 — Base Term
**Owner:** Servicing

Active financing period. Covers: payment processing, account servicing, portfolio and covenant monitoring, add-on schedules.

**Advances when:** Scheduled maturity, or early payoff / buyout.

---

### Stage 8 — End of Term
**Owner:** Servicing + BDO

Exercise the end-of-term option: purchase ($1 or FMV), renewal, return, or TRAC settlement. Final billing.

**Advances when:** Option exercised and settled.

---

### Stage 9 — Maturity Closeout
**Owner:** Ops / Servicing

Final accounting, UCC termination, lien release, and file archive. Win-back handoff returns to the BDO.

**Advances when:** File closed. Cycle returns to prospecting (Stage 1).

## Key Parameters

- **Deal size:** $1MM–$50MM
- **Geography:** US & Canada
- **Borrower revenue range:** $30M–$1B+
- **Collateral type:** Equipment- or project-backed
- **Excluded sectors:** Cannabis, AR, inventory, real estate
- **Funding timeline:** 2–4 weeks (origination phase)
- **Supported structures:** EFA, $1 buyout, 10% PUT, FMV lease, TRAC
