3. **A new demand signal: the self-serve, individual-rep-paid "market intelligence dashboard."** An actual ICP-archetype says reps/VPs would pay $50–100/mo (or $1K + $50/mo) *out of pocket* for a tool that finds/enriches deals and gives them an edge over peers (promotion incentive — "B2P," business-to-person).
