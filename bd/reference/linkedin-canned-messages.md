# LinkedIn Canned Messages — Connection Accept Replies

Quick-fire templates for replying to accepted LinkedIn connections. Swap `[Name]` and send. All follow Alek's direct style: who we are, what we build, design partner framing, call ask.

**Source segments**: `tokenrip.com/s/294dc15c` (GTM filter) + `agents/closer/insurance-linkedin-outreach.md` (insurance bucketing rubric).

**Rules**: One paragraph intro + one paragraph design-partner frame + call ask. No "curious" open. No passive language. No flattery. Keep it under 80 words.
