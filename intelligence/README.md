## The Core Question

> Do agents need financial infrastructure today, and is the gap real — or is the thesis directionally right but too early?

## Success Criteria

4 of 6 hypotheses tested with evidence-based answers by March 14. Including answers we don't like.

## Kill Criteria

If hypotheses #1, #2, and #3 all come back negative — agents can't install skills, operators don't care about policy control, agents can't transact meaningfully — the thesis has a timeline problem.
