## Terminology Note (added v4.1)

**"Bridge monthly" is internal vocabulary only.** It is useful conceptual shorthand for us to describe the structural role of the $500/month — a holding pattern between Phase 1 and Phase 2, not a steady-state subscription. To a customer (especially a small immigration firm), "bridge" reads as finance jargon and requires explanation we should not have to provide.

**In customer-facing copy** (proposal, term sheet, cover email): use plain descriptive language — "Monthly after the pilot," "the $500/month," "post-pilot monthly." Let the structural meaning (12-month lock OR until Phase 2 starts) be carried inline by the explanation, not by a metaphor label.

**In internal docs** (patterns.md, context.md, landscape.md, this inbox note): "bridge" stays — it is faster to write and reason about. The split is: how we think about it ≠ how we describe it to the customer.
