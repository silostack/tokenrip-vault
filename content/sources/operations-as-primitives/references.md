## Primary Sources (researched)

### Theory VC — SaaS Might Be Dead, But Primitives Aren't
- **URL:** https://theoryvc.com/blog-posts/saas-might-be-dead-but-primitives-arent
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-29
- **Key content:** Core thesis: while SaaS products face obsolescence from AI agents, the underlying primitives become more valuable. Three categories of agent-native primitives: data models with embedded constraints, workflow engines encoding operational expertise, and UI components around domain semantics. Key distinction from SDKs: primitives encode invariants ("won't let you build it wrong"), declare composition patterns, and provide machine-readable intent. "Encoding best practices into reusable abstractions is always more efficient than re-deriving them."

### Tom Tunguz — AI's Bundling Moment
- **URL:** https://tomtunguz.com/2026-03-24-saas-unbundled-ai-rebundled/
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-29
- **Key content:** Counterargument to unbundling thesis: AI is rebundling, not unbundling. "When models change every 42 days, buyers can't assemble a best-of-breed stack." Enterprises want platform trust for 3-5 years. Harvey expanded from legal AI to professional services broadly. "The SaaS playbook rewarded specialization. The AI playbook rewards breadth." Useful as a counterpoint — rebundling is real but happening at the primitive layer, not the feature bundle layer.

### Andreessen Horowitz — Platforms vs Verticals and the Next Great Unbundling
- **URL:** https://a16z.com/platforms-vs-verticals-and-the-next-great-unbundling/
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-29
- **Key content:** Classic unbundling pattern: broad platforms face disruption from specialized verticals. Craigslist → specialized marketplaces, YouTube → Twitch/TikTok/MasterClass. "Broad horizontal platforms eventually break. They become a victim of their own success." Pre-AI framing but establishes the unbundling pattern that SaaS-to-primitives extends.

### CB Insights — Unbundling Craigslist
- **URL:** https://www.cbinsights.com/research/craigslist-unbundling/
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-29
- **Key content:** $8.87B raised by startups unbundling Craigslist. Four went public. Establishes the precedent: a bundle of undifferentiated features gets decomposed by specialists.

### Datafi — Beyond CRM: The Future Belongs to an Operating System
- **URL:** https://datafi.co/blog/beyond-crm-why-the-future-of-enterprise-ai-belongs-to-an-operating-system-not-a-/
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-29
- **Key content:** CRM-native AI is structurally limited: "An AI agent without complete context is like a brilliant analyst who has only ever read the sales team's notes." Decomposes CRM into bounded components: data scope (Salesforce records only), workflow access (sales/service only), governance (supplemental). Argues for an AI operating system with complete data integration, vertical integration eliminating seams, and structural governance.

### Start Your Business Magazine — 9 Ways SMEs Are Replacing SaaS with AI Agents
- **URL:** https://www.startyourbusinessmag.com/blog/2026/04/17/9-ways-smes-are-already-replacing-saas-with-ai-agents-and-how-to-do-it
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-29
- **Key content:** Concrete examples of SaaS replacement: PM (AI agents track progress from conversations, auto-update tasks), CRM (capture/enrich/qualify leads automatically), support (first-line query handling with escalation), analytics (aggregate across platforms, respond to natural language), marketing (generate/schedule/analyze across consolidated stack). SMEs saving £10K+/year on SaaS tools.
