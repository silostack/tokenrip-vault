# Sources — Your Agent Doesn't Need a CRM

## Primary Sources (researched)

### Pragmatic Coders — SaaS Is Dead: How AI Agents Reduced SaaS to Just an API
- **URL:** https://www.pragmaticcoders.com/blog/saas-is-dead-how-ai-agents-reduced-saas-to-just-an-api
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-28

### Deloitte — SaaS Meets AI Agents: Transforming Budgets, Customer Experience, and Workforce Dynamics
- **URL:** https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/insights/industry/technology/technology-media-and-telecom-predictions/2026/saas-ai-agents.html

- **Key content:** 57% of respondents investing 21-50% of digital transformation budgets in AI automation. 75% of companies expected to invest in agentic AI in 2026. 83% of AI-native SaaS companies use usage-based pricing. Gartner: 40% of enterprise SaaS spend shifts to usage/outcome-based pricing by 2030.

### Bain & Company — Will Agentic AI Disrupt SaaS?
- **URL:** https://www.bain.com/insights/will-agentic-ai-disrupt-saas-technology-report-2025/
- **Type:** industry_report
- **Captured:** 2026-04-28
- **Key content:** Within three years, any routine rules-based digital task moves from "human plus app" to "AI agent plus API." Vulnerability where high task automation potential meets high AI penetration potential. Gartner: 35% of point-product SaaS tools replaced by agents by 2030. Three-layer stack: systems of record, agent operating systems, outcome interfaces.

### Uncover Alpha — The Great SaaS Unbundling: Why AI Will Destroy Half the Industry

### Superwise — The Rise of AI Agents: Moving Beyond Dashboards
- **URL:** https://superwise.ai/blog/ai-agents-beyond-dashboards/
- **Type:** blog_post
- **Captured:** 2026-04-28
- **Key content:** Dashboards as "rearview mirrors — telling teams what happened, but offering little help on what to do next." Agent architecture: one agent retrieves, another cleans, another analyzes, another recommends. Shift from descriptive analytics to prescriptive intelligence.
